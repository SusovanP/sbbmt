SBB Memorial Trust

Sw. Bindyachal Bajpayee, was a person, throughout his life he dedicated his time for Society, betterment of human life and for helping poor. There are many areas of human Life where hecontributed a lot. He was truly a man one must keep him as an idol for the Entire life.

His son, Late Baccha Bajpayee, was inspired with this, and had initiated actions of Opening a trust in the name of his father. Later his grandson, Shri. Harendra Kumar Bajpayee, who too was inspired a lot with his grandfather’s good work, and he took up the task of trust and started many activities. Even in his personal life, social work was his aim, was his hobby, and was his passion. SBBMT is NEEM certified organization in India.
